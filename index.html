Who We Are

About Skyline Bridge Collective

Skyline Bridge Collective is a consulting and agency group headquartered in Dubai. From there, we develop event and entertainment concepts, bring them to life, and license them to partners in other cities — alongside consulting, marketing and access to our network. Like the bridge in our name, we connect Dubai with our partner cities across Europe.

Where a concept is delivered outside the UAE, our role is limited to consulting, licensing, marketing and brokerage — delivery on the ground is handled by local organizers and licensing partners.
